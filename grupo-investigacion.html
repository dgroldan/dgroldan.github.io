About Our Group

Quantum computing is radically transforming the way we tackle problems impossible for classical computers. Our group work in the development of advanced mathematical models powered by quantum computing. We transform complex problems in industry and science into mathematical formulations that leverage quantum advantage. Our expertise combines differential equations, optimization, and artificial intelligence with quantum algorithms to solve classically intractable modeling challenges.

Research Lines

  • Quantum Computing: Research on quantum algorithms and applications
  • Artificial Intelligence: Machine learning and deep learning applications
  • Image Processing: Advanced techniques in image analysis and processing
  • Numerical Analysis: Development of numerical methods for complex problems
